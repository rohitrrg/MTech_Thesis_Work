# ACM Formatting Checklist

This document is a checklist to ensure the final paper conforms to the standards for submission to an ACM conference or journal.

## 1. Title and Authors
- [ ] **Title:** Is the title in Title Case?
- [ ] **Authors:** Are all authors listed with their affiliations and email addresses?
- [ ] **Anonymization:** For double-blind review, has all identifying author information been removed?

## 2. Abstract and CCS Concepts
- [ ] **Abstract:** Is the abstract a single paragraph?
- [ ] **CCS Concepts:** Have the correct Computing Classification System (CCS) concepts been generated and included?
- [ ] **Keywords:** Are there 3-5 relevant keywords?

## 3. Body Text
- [ ] **Columns:** Is the text formatted in two columns?
- [ ] **Font:** Is the font Times New Roman (or a specified equivalent)? Is the font size correct (usually 9pt or 10pt)?
- [ ] **Headings:** Are section, subsection, and subsubsection headings formatted correctly (e.g., numbering, font weight, spacing)?
- [ ] **Justification:** Is the text fully justified?

## 4. Figures and Tables
- [ ] **Captions:** Does every figure and table have a numbered caption? (e.g., "Figure 1:", "Table 1:")
- [ ] **Placement:** Are figures and tables placed at the top or bottom of a column/page, not in the middle of text?
- [ ] **Resolution:** Are all figures high-resolution (e.g., 300 DPI) and legible?
- [ ] **Referencing:** Is every figure and table referenced in the text (e.g., "as shown in Figure 1...")?

## 5. Citations and References
- [ ] **Style:** Is the citation style correct (e.g., `[1]`, `[1, 2]`, `[1-3]`)?
- [ ] **Completeness:** Does every citation in the text correspond to an entry in the References section?
- [ ] **Formatting:** Is the References section formatted correctly according to ACM style (e.g., author names, year, title, publication venue)?

## 6. Page Numbers and Headers
- [ ] **Page Numbers:** Are page numbers included? (Note: Some templates handle this automatically).
- [ ] **Running Headers:** Are the correct running headers (e.g., conference name, author names) present?

## 7. Final Checks
- [ ] **Proofreading:** Has the entire paper been proofread for spelling and grammar errors?
- [ ] **PDF Validation:** Does the final PDF pass the ACM's PDF validation checks?
- [ ] **Page Limit:** Does the paper adhere to the specified page limit?
